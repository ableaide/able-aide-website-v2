Our NDIS Services

We provide a range of NDIS supports designed to be respectful, reliable and tailored to each person and family. Our focus is on real-life outcomes – not just ticking boxes.

From daily living support to community access and support coordination, we work with you to design supports that make sense in your everyday life.

How We Support You

Our services are designed to work together. You might only need one type of support, or you may benefit from a combination – such as support coordination plus core supports.

Personal Care & Daily Living

Gentle, respectful support with bathing, dressing, meals, mobility, routines, medication prompts and other daily tasks – always focused on dignity and choice.

Learn more →

Community Access & Social Participation

Support to get out into the community – for appointments, shopping, exercise, social activities, faith events and more – at your pace and comfort level.

Learn more →

Support Coordination

Experienced support coordination to help you understand your plan, find providers, coordinate services, manage issues and prepare for NDIS plan reviews.

Learn more →

Allied Health Access

Practical assistance to connect with occupational therapists, physios, speech therapists and other allied health providers – and to follow through on recommendations.

Learn more →

Family & Early Support

Support for young children and their families with daily routines, participation, early development and implementing strategies from therapy in everyday life.

Learn more →

How Our Services Fit Together

1

We understand your whole situation

We look at your daily life, your goals, your family context and your current supports to see where we can genuinely add value.

2

We match supports to your goals

Together, we decide which services are most helpful right now – and how often you’d like them, within your NDIS budget.

3

We review and adjust

As things change – your health, your goals, your family situation – we review supports and adjust the approach where possible.

Funding & Where We Work

NDIS funding types we work with

  • Self-managed NDIS plans
  • Plan-managed NDIS plans
  • Support Coordination (Capacity Building)
  • Core Supports – Daily Life & Social Participation
  • Relevant Capacity Building supports (depending on goals)

Areas we support

  • Melbourne Metro
  • Geelong
  • Ballarat
  • Bendigo
  • Shepparton
  • Regional Victoria

If you live outside these areas, please get in touch – we may still be able to support you or provide guidance.

Not Sure Which Service You Need?
