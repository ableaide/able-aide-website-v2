Our services

What we provide

All supports are person-centred, NDIS-funded and built around your goals — not a generic roster.

Plan management & coordination

Plan Management Australia-wide

Invoice processing, budget tracking and clear reporting — available for participants anywhere in Australia. You keep choice and control without the admin.

Learn more →

Support Coordination Melbourne

Level 1 & Level 2 Support Coordination — understand your plan, connect with the right providers and keep your supports running smoothly.

Learn more →
Housing & independent living

Supported Independent Living (SIL) Melbourne

Daily support in your own home — personal care, meals, routines and household tasks. 24/7 and drop-in options available, built around your goals.

Learn more →

Specialist Disability Accommodation (SDA) Melbourne

Purpose-built NDIS-funded housing for participants with very high support needs. Learn about the four design categories and how to access SDA.

Learn more →
Core supports & specialist services

Psychosocial Recovery Coaching Melbourne

NDIS recovery coaching for people living with mental health conditions — building skills, resilience and community connection.

Learn more →

Personal Care & Daily Living Melbourne

Showering, meals, mobility, routines and home care — always at your pace, built around your comfort and preferences.

Learn more →

Community Participation Melbourne

Appointments, shopping, social activities, faith-based programs and community connection — supported calmly and at your pace.

Learn more →

Early & Family Support Melbourne & VIC

Gentle, family-centred support for children and early years — routines, participation and everyday skills that fit into real family life.

Learn more →

Allied Health Access Melbourne

Connecting with OTs, physios, speech pathologists and other therapists — assessments, reports and ongoing programs.

Learn more →
