What personal care and daily living support can include

Supports are tailored to your goals, routines and preferences. We work respectfully, at your pace, and only provide what you want and consent to.

Showering & hygiene

Support with showering, bathing, grooming, oral care and getting ready for the day.

Dressing & routines

Help with dressing, morning routines and comfort preferences.

Mobility assistance

Transfers, walking aids, positioning and safe support with movement (within scope).

Meals & nutrition

Meal preparation, feeding support and daily living skills (as appropriate).

Medication prompts

Non-clinical reminders to support routines (not medication administration).

Household tasks

Light domestic support like tidying, laundry and maintaining a safe home environment.

Personal Care FAQs

Can I choose the type of support and who provides it?

Yes. We work with your preferences and routines. Supports are always consent-led and respectful.

Do you support self-managed and plan-managed participants?

Yes. We support self-managed, plan-managed and NDIA-managed participants (where applicable).

Do you provide medication administration?

We can provide non-clinical medication prompts and reminders. We do not provide medication administration unless agreed and appropriately covered.
