Supported independent living (SIL)

SIL is NDIS-funded support that helps you live as independently as possible — in your own home or in shared accommodation — with daily assistance provided by trained support workers around your routines and goals.

SIL is suitable for participants with higher support needs who need regular, ongoing assistance with daily living tasks. It's funded under Core Supports in your NDIS plan.

Yes. Learn more about our SIL services →

Psychosocial recovery coaching

A Psychosocial Recovery Coach (PRC) supports people living with mental health conditions to build daily living skills, connect with services and work toward their recovery goals. It is a specialist support funded through your NDIS plan under Improved Daily Living.

A Support Coordinator focuses on implementing your whole NDIS plan and connecting you with providers. A Psychosocial Recovery Coach focuses specifically on your mental health recovery — building skills, resilience and connection to the right supports over time. Some participants have both.

Plan Management

Yes. Able Aide provides Plan Management Australia-wide. Email planmanagement@ableaide.com.au to get started.

Our Plan Management service includes:

  • Processing and paying invoices on your behalf
  • Tracking your NDIS budgets across support categories
  • Providing regular spending summaries
  • Supporting you to use both registered and non-registered providers (where allowed)
  • Clear, prompt communication at every step

Yes. If your NDIS plan includes Plan Management funding, you can switch plan managers at any time. Email planmanagement@ableaide.com.au and we'll guide you through the steps — it's simpler than most people expect.

Providers can email invoices directly to planmanagement@ableaide.com.au. We'll share invoice requirements and processing timeframes once you're onboarded.

If you want Plan Management added, you can request it at your next plan review. Speak with your Support Coordinator or contact us — we can help you understand the options.
