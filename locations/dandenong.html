NDIS Disability Support in Dandenong & South-East Melbourne

Able Aide provides warm, person-centred disability support to participants living in Dandenong and surrounding south-east suburbs. We focus on calm, consistent workers, clear communication and support that respects your home, culture and family.

Whether you need personal care, community access, a new support coordinator or early support for children, we're here to walk alongside you.

Person-centred disability support in Dandenong

The Dandenong region is one of Melbourne’s most culturally diverse communities. We understand that families have unique routines, communication styles, sensory needs and cultural expectations — and our support adapts to those needs.

We focus on building trust, matching workers thoughtfully and communicating clearly with families, carers and support coordinators.

NDIS supports we offer in Dandenong

Personal care & daily living

Respectful support for showering, dressing, meals, mobility, household tasks and routines.

Community access

Support to attend appointments, therapy, shopping, programs, mosque/church/temple, social events and everyday community activities.

Support coordination

Helping you understand your plan, connect with local providers, solve issues and prepare for NDIS reviews.

Family & early support

Calm, practical routines and community engagement for young children and overwhelmed families.

Allied health access

We assist with finding therapists, booking, follow-up and implementing therapy strategies (within scope).
