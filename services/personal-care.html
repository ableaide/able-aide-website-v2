Personal Care & Daily Living Support

Personal care is deeply personal. We support you with kindness, dignity and cultural awareness — always at your pace, never rushed.

From showering, grooming and dressing to mobility support, meals and daily routines, our team takes time to understand your preferences and comfort.

Support that respects your comfort

We focus on communication, consistency and trust — especially during sensitive routines such as showering or dressing.

Talk to us about your needs

Support Designed Around Your Daily Life

Daily tasks can take significant physical, emotional or sensory energy. Whether you need guidance, encouragement, full support or routine-based assistance, we adjust to your abilities, communication style and comfort level.

We understand the importance of privacy, cultural preferences and maintaining dignity during intimate care. Our workers meet you where you are — providing reassurance, safety and a calm environment.

What We Can Help With

Personal Hygiene & Care

Respectful support that keeps you safe, clean and comfortable.

  • Showering, bathing or sponge baths
  • Toileting & continence care
  • Oral hygiene
  • Hair washing, shaving & grooming
  • Skin care & basic pressure care
  • Applying lotions & non-clinical creams

Dressing, Mobility & Transfers

Safe mobility support tailored to your physical needs.

  • Dressing & undressing
  • Choosing weather-appropriate clothing
  • Safe manual handling & transfers
  • Using walkers, hoists & mobility aids
  • Positioning for comfort
  • Morning & evening routines

Daily Living & Home Support

Helping create comfort, safety and routine at home.

  • Meal preparation & light cooking
  • Light household tasks (tidy, dishes, laundry)
  • Linen changes & bed assistance
  • Medication reminders (non-clinical)
  • Following therapist strategies
  • Supporting home organisation

Our Approach to Personal Care

Exceptional personal care is defined by HOW support is delivered — gently, respectfully and with an understanding of your routines and preferences.

1

We Listen First

We learn your preferences — water temperature, products, routines, and which tasks you want independence with.

2

We Work at Your Pace

Some days you may want to move slowly. Others more independently. We adapt each visit to your comfort.

3

We Provide Consistency

Wherever possible, the same support workers attend — making routines predictable, especially for sensory needs or anxiety.

Who This Service Is For

This service may be suitable if you:

  • Need support with showering, mobility or hygiene
  • Experience fatigue, chronic pain or limited mobility
  • Live alone and want daily support
  • Have sensory differences and need a gentle approach
  • Are recovering from injury or surgery
  • Are working on independence goals
  • Have carers who need respite

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I choose my own support worker?

Yes — matching matters. You can request preferences in gender, communication, cultural background and more.

Is personal care funded by the NDIS?

Yes, under Core Supports — “Assistance with Daily Life.”

Can I change workers?

Absolutely. Your comfort always comes first.

Do you support people with complex needs?

Yes — including mobility challenges, intellectual disability, autism and more.

Will workers follow my therapy plans?

Yes — we can follow OT/physio/speech recommendations at home.

Are workers trained in manual handling?

Yes — all workers receive safe transfer & mobility training.

Do you support children?

Yes — we offer age-appropriate routines for children and teens.

Can you help after hospital discharge?

Yes — we support transitions from hospital to home.

Do I need to prepare my home?

No — support is realistic, not perfection-based.

What if I feel embarrassed?

Many participants feel this way initially. Our workers help build comfort and trust.

Need Respectful Help with Daily Living?

We understand how personal this support can be. If you’d like to talk through options, funding or next steps, we’re here to help.
