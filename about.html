About Able Aide

Able Aide was created with one purpose — to deliver disability support that is truly person-centred, culturally respectful and built on trust. We believe in consistent workers, meaningful relationships and support that adapts to your life, not the other way around.

Our team works across Victoria to support participants in building independence, confidence and wellbeing. We collaborate closely with families, support coordinators and therapists to ensure every participant receives the best possible care.

Syeda Zahra

Founder & Director – Able Aide

Syeda has over 10 years of experience in the disability sector, working across support work, case coordination, family advocacy and community engagement. Her passion for empowering participants led her to establish Able Aide with a focus on compassion, dignity and cultural understanding.

She believes every participant deserves meaningful support, consistent workers and a team that listens deeply. Syeda’s leadership ensures Able Aide remains committed to quality, safety and personalised care.

Our Values

Respect

We honour your culture, identity, preferences and routines. Support should feel safe, familiar and inclusive.

Consistency

Participants thrive with regular and reliable support workers. We prioritise long-term relationships and stable rosters.

Empowerment

Our job is to support you to live the life you choose — with independence, confidence and the right tools.

Cultural Understanding

We understand the importance of language, faith and family roles. Our services are sensitive to cultural needs and values.

Quality & Safety

All workers follow NDIS quality and safeguarding requirements, ensuring high-standard, safe and professional care.

Collaboration

We work closely with families, coordinators and therapists to ensure participants have the best support team possible.
