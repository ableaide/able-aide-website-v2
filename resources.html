NDIS Resources & Guides

The NDIS can feel complex. These resources are designed to give you clearer, more practical information – especially if you’re supporting a family member or navigating the system for the first time.

This page is general in nature and does not replace professional advice or official NDIS information.

Need help with your specific plan?

If you’d like support tailored to your situation, our team can walk through your plan, your goals and your options.

Popular Topics

Understanding NDIS funding types

Learn the difference between self-managed, plan-managed and agency-managed funding, and how each option changes the way you choose and pay for services.

Tip: If you’re not sure which one is right for you, talk with your support coordinator, plan manager or LAC before making changes.

Preparing for your NDIS planning meeting

Ideas for thinking about your daily routines, support needs, future goals and what’s currently difficult – so you can clearly explain this at planning or review meetings.

Working well with support workers

Suggestions for building a positive relationship with support workers – including communication, boundaries, giving feedback and what to expect from a professional service.

Support for families & carers

Information for parents, partners and carers about looking after yourself, asking for help, and making use of respite or support services where available.

Advocacy and speaking up

How to raise concerns, ask for reviews, request changes from providers or seek advocacy support if you feel your voice isn’t being heard.

Staying organised

Practical tips for keeping track of appointments, reports, receipts and communication with providers so reviews feel less stressful.
