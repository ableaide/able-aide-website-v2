Professional NDIS Support in Point Cook

Able Aide is proud to offer comprehensive NDIS support services to the Point Cook community. Our experienced team provides person-centred care that helps you achieve your goals.

Our Point Cook NDIS Services

  • Daily Living Support: Personal care, meal preparation, household tasks, and medication support
  • Community Participation: Support to access local parks, shopping, recreation facilities, and community events
  • Social and Recreation Activities: Inclusive activities at local venues and facilities
  • Transport Assistance: Help getting to appointments, shopping, and community activities
  • Life Skills Development: Building independence and confidence in your own community

Why Choose Able Aide in Point Cook?

Point Cook is a vibrant, growing coastal suburb with diverse cultural communities and excellent facilities. Our local knowledge means we can support you to access local services and stay connected with your community.
