Allied Health Access Support

Finding therapists, organising assessments and managing waitlists can feel overwhelming. We help participants and families across Victoria access occupational therapy, physiotherapy, speech therapy, psychology and other NDIS-recommended specialists.

Our role is to make the process clearer, calmer and easier — supporting you to get the assessments, therapy and reports you need.

Support connecting with therapists who fit your needs

We help bridge the gap between therapy recommendations and real life — assisting with access, communication, organisation and implementing strategies at home.

Making Allied Health Easier to Navigate

Different disciplines, waitlists, forms and NDIS requirements can be confusing. We help you connect with therapists, understand their recommendations and keep everything organised.

We are not a clinic — our role is to support you to access therapy services that match your needs and goals, and help integrate strategies into your everyday routines.

How We Support Allied Health Access

Finding the Right Therapists

We help you connect with allied health professionals across Victoria:

  • Occupational Therapists (OT)
  • Physiotherapists
  • Speech Pathologists
  • Psychologists & counsellors
  • Behaviour support practitioners
  • Assessors & specialists relevant to your NDIS goals

Organising Appointments & Assessments

We support the practical side of accessing therapy:

  • Enquiries & navigating waitlists
  • Basic intake & referral forms (within scope)
  • Booking & confirming appointments
  • Coordinating transport or support worker attendance
  • Tracking dates, reports & follow-ups

Using Therapy Strategies at Home

We help bring therapy into everyday life:

  • Integrating strategies into routines
  • Simple prompts or visuals (where appropriate)
  • Ensuring support workers understand recommendations
  • Supporting consistent use of equipment
  • Preparing for NDIS reassessments

Who This Service Is For

Allied Health Access may be helpful if you:

  • Don't know where to start finding therapists
  • Are stuck on multiple waitlists
  • Feel overwhelmed by reports or recommendations
  • Struggle to track appointments & follow-ups
  • Need help implementing therapy strategies
  • Have upcoming NDIS reviews that require assessments

Funding & Locations

  • Typically funded through Capacity Building or Core
  • Self-managed & plan-managed participants
  • Available across Melbourne & regional Victoria

If you’re unsure how this fits into your plan, we can help you check.

Our Approach to Working With Therapists

Good therapy outcomes happen when communication is clear and everyone is aligned.

Participant-Centred

Your preferences guide everything — from choosing therapists to planning routines.

Collaborative

With your consent, we can communicate with your therapists, GP, school or Support Coordinator to help keep everyone aligned.

Practical & Realistic

We help break recommendations into small, achievable steps that fit your real life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you an allied health clinic?

No — we help you access therapists, but we don’t deliver therapy ourselves.

Can you recommend therapists?

Yes — we provide options based on your needs, location and preferences.

Can you book appointments for me?

Yes — we support enquiries, bookings and coordinating schedules.

Do you attend therapy sessions?

Sometimes — where helpful, appropriate and funded.

Can you help with NDIS reports?

We track due dates & follow-ups. Therapists complete the clinical reports.

Is this the same as Support Coordination?

Some tasks overlap. We can help determine what fits your plan best.

Do you support equipment & AT?

We assist with logistics, but clinical decisions remain with therapists.

What if I already have therapists?

We help coordinate between providers and put recommendations into daily routines.

Do you support participants with limited English?

Yes — we work with interpreters and culturally appropriate services.

How do I start?

Contact us directly or through your existing providers.
