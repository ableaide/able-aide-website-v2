Reliable NDIS support across Melbourne

Melbourne’s inner suburbs are fast-paced and diverse — and participants often tell us they need support that is consistent, respectful and flexible around their lives. Able Aide provides person-centred support with a focus on culture, communication and safety.

NDIS supports available in Melbourne

Personal care & daily living

Showering, dressing, routines, meals, mobility, domestic assistance and safe daily support.

Community access

Transport, appointments, social activities, education, wellness, recreation and independence-building.

Support coordination

Understanding your plan, choosing providers, solving issues and preparing for reviews.

Family & early supports

Help with routines, behaviour, communication, participation and therapy follow-through.

Allied health access

Finding therapists, booking assessments, managing waitlists and integrating therapy strategies.
