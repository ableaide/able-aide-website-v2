Understanding NDIS supports in Melbourne

Melbourne has a wide range of NDIS-funded supports. The best outcomes come from supports that match the participant's routines, communication preferences, culture, and goals.

Core supports

Help with daily living, personal care, and community participation, depending on needs and goals.

Capacity Building

Supports that build independence, skills, and confidence — often delivered alongside therapists and coordinators.

Allied health access

Help coordinating therapy appointments and accessing evidence and reports when appropriate.

Melbourne areas commonly supported

Participants and families access NDIS supports across Greater Melbourne, including:

  • Frankston & Mornington Peninsula
  • Dandenong & south-east suburbs
  • Ringwood & eastern suburbs
  • Epping & northern suburbs
  • Werribee & western suburbs
  • Sunshine & Brimbank area
  • Melbourne CBD & inner suburbs

Availability varies depending on support needs and worker capacity. Contact us and we'll confirm quickly.

Choosing a support provider in Melbourne

  • Clear, timely communication with families and coordinators
  • Consistent support workers matched to preferences
  • Respect for culture, routines and communication style
  • Transparent pricing aligned with the NDIS Price Guide
  • Safe worker screening and professional boundaries

How Able Aide can support you

Able Aide provides warm, person-centred disability support across Melbourne for self-managed and plan-managed participants. We focus on calm, respectful supports that fit real life — at home and in the community.
