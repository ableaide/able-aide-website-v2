Refer an NDIS Participant

Support coordinators, plan managers, allied health professionals, community workers and families can use this form to refer an NDIS participant to Able Aide for disability support.

We aim to respond promptly, with clear communication and realistic expectations about capacity.

Who can refer?

  • Support Coordinators & Specialist Support Coordinators
  • Plan Managers & LACs
  • Therapists & Allied Health Providers
  • Schools & Community Workers
  • Parents, Guardians & Carers
  • Participants (self-referral)

Referral Form

Please provide as much relevant information as possible. This helps us understand the participant’s supports, routines, location and safety considerations before making contact.

What Happens Next?

1

We review the referral

We review supports requested, location, risk information and funding type to determine suitability and current capacity.

2

We confirm capacity

If we can proceed, we contact the referrer and/or participant. If we cannot accept, we communicate this honestly.

3

We begin intake

We gather additional info if needed, discuss goals and preferences, and plan supports or support coordination together.
