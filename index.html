How We Support You

Every person and family is different. We take time to understand your needs, routines, culture, communication style and goals so that support feels respectful, calm and practical — not rushed or overwhelming.

Personal Care & Daily Living

Support with daily routines such as showering, dressing, meals, mobility and hygiene — always delivered with dignity, consent and at your pace.

Why Families & Participants Choose Able Aide

Experience You Can Trust

Led by Syeda, with over 10 years of experience in disability support, support coordination and advocacy. We understand the NDIS and the realities of daily life.

Consistency & Reliability

We know how important familiar faces are. We aim for consistent support workers and honest communication about our capacity, so you know what to expect.

Respect for Culture & Family

We respect your culture, faith, language, pronouns, routines and family structure. We ask before we assist and work in a way that aligns with your values.

NDIS Services We Offer

Personal Care & Daily Living

Community Access & Social Participation

Help to attend appointments, shopping, activities and social outings safely.

We support NDIS participants across Victoria, including:

  • Melbourne Metro
  • Geelong
  • Ballarat

If you live outside these areas, please contact us — we are often flexible and may still be able to help or suggest alternatives.

Funding We Work With

  • Self-managed NDIS participants
  • Plan-managed NDIS participants
  • Families and carers (in partnership with the NDIS plan)

If you’re unsure how your funding works, we can explain in simple, clear language.

Who We Support

Adults

Adults needing support at home, in the community or with coordinating their NDIS plan. We can work directly with you, your family, or your Support Coordinator.

Children & Young People

Children and teenagers with NDIS plans, with a strong focus on family routines, participation, emotional safety and working alongside early childhood and school supports.

Families, Carers & Support Coordinators

Families and carers who are feeling stretched, and Support Coordinators looking for a reliable, transparent support provider for participants in Victoria.

Getting Started Is Simple

1

Enquire

Get in touch and tell us about your situation, location and NDIS funding. You can contact us directly or be referred by a Support Coordinator, planner or provider.

2

Meet & Plan

We arrange a time to meet (in person or online) with you and, if you like, your family or Support Coordinator, to talk through your goals and what good support looks like for you.

3

Start Supports

If we’re the right fit, we agree on a plan and start supports at your pace — checking in as we go and adjusting where possible as your needs change.
