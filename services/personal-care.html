Personal Care & Daily Living

Every person deserves care that feels calm, respectful and aligned with their routines. Our personal care supports are delivered with dignity, cultural awareness and patience.

Whether you need help once a week or multiple times a day, we work at your pace and ensure you feel safe, comfortable and in control.

  • Delivered by trusted, police-checked & WWCC-verified staff
  • Support for children, adults & older participants
  • Self-managed & plan-managed participants welcome

What Personal Care Includes

Our team provides gentle, respectful assistance tailored to your physical, cultural and personal needs. You stay in control â€” we simply support you in the way that feels right for you.

Showering & hygiene

Support with showering, bathing, grooming, oral care and getting ready for the day.

Dressing & routines

Help with getting dressed, morning routines and comfort preferences.

Mobility assistance

Transfers, walking aids, positioning, hoists and safe manual handling.

Meals & nutrition

Meal prep, feeding assistance and practicing independent living skills.

Medication prompts

Non-clinical reminders to support safety and routine.

Household tasks

Light domestic support including tidying, laundry and maintaining a safe home environment.
