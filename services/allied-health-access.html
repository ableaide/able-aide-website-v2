Allied Health Access

Supporting you to access the right Allied Health therapists, assessments and reports — so you can achieve meaningful progress and reach your NDIS goals.

What Allied Health Access Includes

We help participants connect with qualified therapists and organise assessments, reports and ongoing support. Whether you need early intervention, mobility support, communication therapy or functional assessments — we’re here to help.

  • Occupational Therapy (OT)
  • Physiotherapy
  • Speech Therapy
  • Psychology & behavioural support referrals
  • Functional Capacity Assessments (FCA)
  • Assistive Technology (AT) reports
  • Home modifications assessments
  • Early intervention therapy support
  • Coordination of therapy appointments
  • Connecting you with trusted therapy providers

Therapy that fits your goals

Every participant has unique needs — we help you access therapists who specialise in your disability, age group and goals.

We also support you through the process of referrals, assessments, reports and follow-up appointments.

Who Is This Service For?

Allied Health Access is suitable for participants of all ages who require therapeutic support, assessments or capacity-building interventions.

Children

Early intervention, developmental support & therapy referrals.

Adults

Therapy for mobility, daily living, communication & independence.

Aging Participants

Support with mobility, home modifications & safety assessments.

How Able Aide Supports You

Accessing Allied Health can feel overwhelming — especially when coordinating multiple providers. We simplify the process and ensure you receive high-quality, consistent support.

  • We find therapists that match your needs
  • We help organise assessments and reports
  • We support communication between you and providers
  • We help prepare for therapy-related reviews
  • We ensure your therapy aligns with your NDIS goals

A coordinated and supportive approach

We ensure smooth communication between therapists, families, coordinators and other service providers — so therapy is consistent and effective.

Why Choose Able Aide?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide therapy directly?

No — we assist you to access qualified therapy providers through referral pathways.

Can you help with urgent assessments?

Yes. We work with providers who can offer timely assessments when possible.

Do you help with AT and home modification reports?

Yes — we support the process of obtaining the right assessments and documentation.

Do you coordinate appointments?

Yes — we help organise schedules, follow-ups and communication with providers.

Is this service available for children?

Yes — early intervention referrals and supports are fully included.
