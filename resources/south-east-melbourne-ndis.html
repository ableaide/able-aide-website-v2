NDIS supports in South-East Melbourne

Families in south-east Melbourne often use a mix of home-based and community supports. The right mix depends on goals, routines, and what feels safe and sustainable for the participant and their family.

Support coordination in the South-East

Support coordination can help participants and families:

  • Understand plan budgets and what supports can be used for
  • Find suitable providers and coordinate services
  • Prepare for plan reviews and gather evidence
  • Problem-solve when things change or supports break down
