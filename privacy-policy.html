Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Able Aide collects, uses and protects your information under Australian privacy law and NDIS Practice Standards.

Your information is handled with care

We follow the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) and NDIS requirements.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Able Aide manages personal information in accordance with:

  • Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)
  • Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)
  • NDIS Act 2013 & Practice Standards
  • NDIS Code of Conduct

2. What Personal Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

Participants & Families

  • Name, address, contact details
  • Date of birth
  • Health, disability & support needs
  • NDIS plan details
  • Cultural or communication preferences
  • Emergency contacts

Support Coordinators & Referrers

  • Name & organisation
  • Contact and referral details

Website Visitors

  • IP address & device info
  • Pages visited & interactions
  • General location (city-level)

Sensitive Information

Collected only with consent or as required by law:

  • Health & disability info
  • Cultural or linguistic background
  • Religious preferences (if relevant)

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you
  • Website forms (Formspree)
  • Support coordinators
  • Google Analytics (GA4)
  • Meta Pixel

4. Why We Collect Your Information

  • Provide disability support
  • Understand your needs & goals
  • Communicate with you or representatives
  • Meet regulatory requirements

5. Website Analytics & Cookies

Google Analytics (GA4)

  • Page views & interactions
  • Traffic sources
  • Device & browser details

Meta Pixel

  • Ad measurement
  • Retargeting
  • Campaign optimisation

Opt out anytime:

  • Google Analytics opt-out
  • Meta ads preferences

6. Storage & Security

  • Secure digital storage
  • Restricted access
  • Password protection
  • Encrypted cloud systems

Hosting: Website data is securely hosted on Netlify.

7. Sharing Your Information

  • With your nominated representatives
  • Support coordinators or therapists
  • Plan managers
  • When legally required

We never sell your information.

8. Overseas Data Disclosure

Analytics and hosting tools may process data in:

  • USA
  • EU
  • Asia-Pacific regions

You may request:

  • A copy of your information
  • Corrections
  • Preference updates
