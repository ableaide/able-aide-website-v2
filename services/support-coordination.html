Support Coordination

Able Aide provides Support Coordination to help NDIS participants understand their plan, connect with the right providers, navigate the NDIS, and build confidence in managing their supports. Our approach is warm, collaborative and empowering — we work alongside you, your family and your care team to ensure your supports are working effectively.

How we support you

We can help with a wide range of coordination and plan navigation tasks:

  • Understanding your NDIS plan and funding categories
  • Finding and connecting with suitable service providers
  • Building your capacity to manage your supports
  • Strengthening informal and community networks
  • Preparing for plan reassessments & reporting
  • Monitoring service delivery and budgets
  • Troubleshooting issues with providers
  • Coordinating allied health, core supports and community programs
  • Ensuring services are safe, high quality and aligned with your goals

Your coordination specialist

Our Support Coordination team works Victoria-wide, helping participants:

  • Navigate the NDIS with confidence
  • Make informed decisions about supports
  • Develop skills and independence
  • Build a strong, sustainable team around you

We support adults, young people and families across Melbourne, Geelong, Ballarat, Bendigo, Shepparton and surrounding regions.

Levels of Support Coordination We Provide

Level 1 – Support Connection

We help you understand your plan, connect with local providers, and begin using your supports with confidence.

Level 2 – Support Coordination

We coordinate your supports, monitor your progress, assist with budgeting and work collaboratively with your support network.

Specialist Coordination*

For participants with complex needs, we work closely with allied health and behaviour practitioners to stabilise supports. (*Depending on availability)

How Support Coordination Works

1

Meet & understand your goals

We meet with you (and your family, if you choose) to understand your needs, routines and NDIS plan.

2

We help you select the right providers, coordinate schedules, and ensure everything works smoothly.

3

Monitor & grow independence

We support you to develop skills, solve problems and prepare for plan reassessments.

Frequently asked questions

What does a Support Coordinator do?

A Support Coordinator helps you understand and implement your NDIS plan, connect with providers, troubleshoot issues and build capacity to manage your supports independently.

Do you work with all NDIS funding types?

Yes — we support participants who are self-managed and plan-managed.

Can you help prepare for my plan reassessment?

Absolutely. We gather reports, review your progress, identify gaps, and help you feel confident going into your plan meeting.

What areas do you service?

We provide Support Coordination across all of Victoria — including Melbourne, Geelong, Ballarat, Bendigo, Shepparton and surrounding regions.
