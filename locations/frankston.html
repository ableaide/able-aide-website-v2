Person-centred disability support in Frankston

Frankston and the Bayside / Peninsula corridor includes a wide mix of families, single adults, older people and young people building independence. We understand that every home, culture and routine is different – and that good support must adapt to real life, not the other way around.

Able Aide offers calm, reliable support workers who take time to build trust. We’re mindful of sensory needs, mental health, family dynamics and the realities of everyday life – appointments, fatigue, school runs, community activities and hospital visits.

NDIS supports available in Frankston

Personal care & daily living

We provide respectful personal care in Frankston homes – including showering, grooming, dressing, mobility support, meals and medication prompts (non-clinical). Our aim is to support your safety and dignity, while encouraging independence wherever possible.

Community access & appointments

From medical appointments and therapy visits to shopping, community programs and walks along the foreshore, we support you to get out safely and at your pace. For some participants this means short, local outings. For others it means building confidence to re-join community activities.

Support coordination

For participants with funded Support Coordination, we help you understand your plan, connect with local providers, problem-solve issues and prepare for NDIS reassessments. We collaborate closely with Frankston-based services, hospitals and therapists where appropriate.

Family & early support

For families with young children, we support morning and afternoon routines, transitions to kinder or school, community access and using therapy strategies at home. The focus is calm, practical support that reduces stress for the whole family.

Allied health access

We help participants connect with occupational therapists, physiotherapists, speech pathologists, psychologists and other allied health providers. This may include enquiries, managing waitlists, booking appointments and supporting therapy follow-through at home (within our scope).

Where we support around Frankston

Our team actively supports participants in Frankston and nearby suburbs, including:

  • Frankston, Frankston North & Frankston South
  • Seaford & Carrum Downs
  • Langwarrin & Karingal
  • Selected parts of the Mornington Peninsula (please ask)

If you live just outside these areas, we may still be able to assist depending on support hours and worker availability.

Working with support coordinators & local providers

We regularly collaborate with support coordinators, plan managers, therapists, GPs, schools and community services around Frankston and the Peninsula. Communication is clear and realistic – we’re honest about capacity, waitlists and what we can safely provide.

If you’re a local provider or coordinator wanting to explore a referral, you can contact us directly or use our online referral form.
