Personal Care & Daily Living

Able Aide provides gentle, respectful personal care support to help you feel comfortable, confident and safe at home. Our team works alongside you and your family to make daily routines easier, while always protecting your dignity, privacy and choice.

What we can help with

Personal care supports are tailored to each person. Depending on your goals and NDIS plan, we can assist with:

  • Showering, bathing and hygiene
  • Dressing, undressing and choosing clothing
  • Grooming – hair care, shaving, skin care, oral hygiene
  • Toileting and continence support
  • Mobility and transfers (including use of equipment)
  • Morning and bedtime routines
  • Meal preparation and light kitchen tasks
  • Drinks and snacks, prompts to eat and drink
  • Medication prompts (as per support plan)
  • Light domestic support linked to your daily living

Who this service is for

Our Personal Care & Daily Living support is suitable for NDIS participants who:

  • Need hands-on support with personal care tasks
  • Benefit from prompts or supervision for safety
  • Are building independence with daily routines
  • Prefer consistent, familiar workers in their home

We support adults, young people and families across Victoria – including Melbourne, Geelong, Ballarat, Bendigo, Shepparton and surrounding regions.

How personal care support works with Able Aide

1

2

3

Respectful, culturally aware support

Personal care is one of the most sensitive types of support. We know that inviting someone into your home and personal space is a big step.

Our workers are trained to provide care that is:

  • Private and discreet
  • Guided by your preferences and consent
  • Respectful of gender and cultural considerations
  • Delivered at your pace, with clear communication

We also understand the importance of family roles, religious practices and cultural routines. Where possible, we match workers who are a good fit for your language, communication style and household environment.

Frequently asked questions

Can I choose a support worker of a specific gender?

Yes, where possible we respect preferences around worker gender and cultural background for personal care supports. Let us know what feels safest and most comfortable for you.

Do you work with self-managed and plan-managed NDIS?

Yes. Able Aide works with both self-managed and plan-managed NDIS participants across Victoria.

Can you help build my independence?

Absolutely. Our goal is to support you to do as much as you safely can. We can provide coaching, prompts and gradual support so you can build skills and confidence with daily tasks.

What areas do you service for personal care?

We provide in-home personal care across Victoria – including Melbourne metro, Geelong, Ballarat, Bendigo, Shepparton and nearby regional areas. If you are just outside these regions, please contact us to discuss.
