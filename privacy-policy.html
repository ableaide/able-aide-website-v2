Privacy Policy

How Able Aide collects, stores and protects your personal information.

Introduction

Able Aide Pty Ltd (ABN: 116 730 790 73), located in Tarneit, Victoria, is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) and the NDIS Practice Standards. Our NDIS registration is currently in progress, and we maintain the same privacy expectations as registered providers.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and date of birth
  • Address, phone number and email
  • Emergency contacts
  • Support needs and preferences
  • Notes from meetings and communications

Sensitive Information

  • Health and disability information
  • Behavioural or medical history relevant to supports
  • Cultural or language background
  • Details about guardians, nominees or legal representatives

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information directly from you or your authorised representatives, including:

  • Intake forms, calls, emails and meetings
  • Family, carers or guardians (with consent)
  • Support coordinators or allied health professionals
  • During delivery of NDIS support services

3. Why We Collect Information

We collect information to:

  • Provide you with safe, high-quality disability support
  • Understand your needs, goals and routines
  • Communicate with you, your family or representatives
  • Meet legal and duty-of-care requirements
  • Improve our services and ensure quality

4. How We Use and Share Information

Your information may be shared with:

  • Your authorised representatives
  • Support coordinators, plan managers or health professionals
  • Emergency services (if required for safety)

We never sell personal information.

5. Security of Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your data from loss, misuse or unauthorised access. Electronic records are securely stored, and access is restricted to authorised staff.

6. Access and Correction

You may request access to, or correction of, your personal information at any time.

7. Website Data and Cookies

Our website may use cookies or analytics to improve performance. No identifiable personal data is collected without your consent.
