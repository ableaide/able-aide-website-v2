Allied Health Access FAQs

Is allied health access funded by the NDIS?

It may be funded depending on your plan and the support type. Many participants use their plan to access therapy services. Support with organising appointments and communication may be funded under relevant supports where appropriate.

Can you help with reports for plan reviews or equipment?

Yes. We can help you organise appointments and follow up on assessments and reports used for plan reviews, assistive technology and therapy planning, with your consent.

Do you offer telehealth options?

Yes. Where suitable, we can help you access providers who offer telehealth appointments, which can reduce travel and speed up access.
