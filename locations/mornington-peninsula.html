If you're unsure whether we can support your suburb, feel free to contact us — we are happy to check availability.

NDIS services available on the peninsula

Personal care & daily living

Respectful support with showering, grooming, meals, mobility and home routines — always with dignity and patience.

Community access & appointments

Supported outings, beach walks, shopping, programs, worship, activities and appointments with workers who move at your pace.

Support coordination

Helping you understand your plan, connect with the right providers and advocate for what you need.

Allied health access

Assistance accessing OTs, physios, psychologists, speech therapists and assessment providers.

Family & early support

Gentle, practical help for families and young children with routines, communication, therapy support and positive engagement.

Why families choose Able Aide

  • Respectful, culturally aware and family-centred
  • Clear communication with coordinators & families
  • Reliable support workers — no constant rotation
  • Support for children, teens & adults
  • Warm, collaborative and person-centred approach
