Community Access & Social Participation

Being part of the community is essential for wellbeing. Whether it’s attending appointments, joining activities, going shopping, seeing friends or exploring outdoors, community access helps build confidence, independence and connection.

We support participants across Victoria with safe, flexible and personalised community access — always at your pace.

Support that builds confidence

We understand that going out can feel overwhelming. Our support workers walk alongside you — respecting your pace, preferences and comfort.

Support That Helps You Live Life Fully

Community access is about freedom, confidence and engaging in daily life. We support participants with mobility, anxiety, sensory needs, appointments, errands, recreation, independence-building and social connection.

Appointments & Essential Trips

  • GP, specialist and therapy appointments
  • NDIS meetings or provider visits
  • Medication collection & health tasks
  • Bank, post office & essential errands
  • Support with paperwork (within scope)

Shopping & Daily Tasks

  • Grocery shopping & budgeting
  • Buying clothes or essentials
  • Planning shopping lists
  • Money-handling support
  • Transport & navigating stores safely

Social & Recreational Activities

  • Visiting parks, libraries or community centres
  • Joining hobbies, classes or programs
  • Attending cultural or faith events
  • Participating in disability-friendly groups
  • Outdoor walks & fresh air outings

Who This Support Is For

Community access may be right for you if:

  • You want to attend appointments safely
  • You feel anxious or overwhelmed outside
  • You’re building independence with errands
  • You want to join programs or activities
  • You’d like more social connection
  • You need mobility or transport support

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide transport?

Yes — support workers can transport you or assist using public transport.

Can I choose where we go?

Yes — you decide the destinations, goals and pace.

Do you assist with anxiety or sensory needs?

Yes — we support emotional, social, sensory and mobility needs.

Is this funded by the NDIS?

Yes — funded under Core Supports or Capacity Building depending on goals.

Can you attend appointments with me?

Yes — we can accompany you and assist within scope.

Do you support children and teens?

Yes — we provide age-appropriate support for young people.

Can you help me join programs?

Yes — we help research, enquire and support attendance.

How often can I have community access?

As often as your NDIS plan allows.

Can I request a specific type of worker?

Yes — we respect cultural, gender and personal preferences.

Will this help with independence?

Yes — we encourage confidence and skill-building step-by-step.

Ready to Feel More Confident in the Community?

Whether you want to attend appointments, join activities or explore your community, we’re here to support you at your pace.
