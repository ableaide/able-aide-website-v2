NDIS services available in Bendigo

Personal care & daily living

Friendly, respectful support with showering, meals, mobility and daily routines — always delivered with dignity and consistency.

Community access & appointments

Helping you stay connected through supported outings, programs, activities, appointments and social participation.

Support coordination

Guidance to understand your plan, connect with the right providers and prepare for NDIS reassessments and reviews.

Allied health access

Support to access OTs, physios, speech therapists, psychologists and assessment providers without stress or long wait times.

Family & early support

Practical assistance for families navigating routines, behaviour support, early intervention and therapy recommendations.

Why Bendigo participants choose Able Aide

  • Respectful of culture, family & communication preferences
  • Clear, honest and frequent communication
  • Support for children, teenagers & adults
  • Warm, family-oriented philosophy
