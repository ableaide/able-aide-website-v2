Support Coordination

Independent, person-centred support to help you understand your NDIS plan, connect with the right services and stay in control of your goals.

What Support Coordination Includes

Support Coordination helps you build the skills and confidence to use your NDIS plan effectively. Our approach is clear, organised and tailored to your unique needs.

  • Understanding your NDIS plan and funding categories
  • Finding the right service providers
  • Organising supports, appointments and reports
  • Building capacity and independence
  • Monitoring budgets and service agreements
  • Assistance during plan reviews
  • Advocating for your needs and goals
  • Helping you resolve provider or service issues

Clear, supportive guidance

The NDIS can be confusing — but you don’t need to navigate it alone. We take the time to explain your plan in a simple, friendly and helpful way.

With ongoing communication and transparent support, you always stay in control.

Who Is This Service For?

Support Coordination is ideal for participants who want help navigating services, managing plan budgets or connecting with the right supports.

New to the NDIS

If you're feeling unsure about what to do next, we're here to guide you step-by-step.

Complex Needs

Participants with multiple providers, higher support needs or many appointments.

Families & Carers

We work alongside families to organise supports and reduce stress.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with all NDIS plans?

Yes — we support self-managed and plan-managed participants.

How often will I see my Support Coordinator?

As often as you need — weekly, fortnightly or monthly.

Can you help prepare for NDIS reviews?

Absolutely. We assist with reports, goals, evidence and provider updates.

Do you help with resolving provider issues?

Yes — we can mediate, organise changes and help find better options.

Can I choose who I work with?

Yes. You always have choice and control over your Support Coordinator.
