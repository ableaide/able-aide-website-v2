Choosing a support provider in Melbourne

When selecting an NDIS provider, it helps to look for:

  • Clear, timely communication with families and coordinators
  • Consistent support workers matched to preferences
  • Respect for culture, routines, and communication style
  • Transparent pricing aligned with the NDIS Price Guide
  • Safe worker screening and professional boundaries
