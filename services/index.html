Our NDIS Support Services

Able Aide provides a range of NDIS supports designed to help you live a life that feels safe, meaningful and connected. We work across Victoria with self-managed and plan-managed participants, tailoring supports to your goals, routines and culture.

Personal Care & Daily Living

Gentle, respectful in-home support with showering, dressing, grooming, meal preparation, routines and daily living tasks.

Learn more →

Community Access & Social Support

Support to get out into the community — appointments, shopping, social outings, activities, independence and confidence-building.

Learn more →

Support Coordination

Helping you understand your NDIS plan, connect with providers, build your support team and prepare for plan reassessments.

Learn more →

Allied Health Access Support

Assistance to access therapy services, attend appointments, understand recommendations and implement strategies between sessions.

Learn more →

Family & Early Support

Family-centred support for children and young people, including routines, transitions, emotional support and early intervention assistance.

Who we support

Able Aide works with NDIS participants of all ages, families and carers across Victoria, including:

  • Children and young people
  • Adults with physical, intellectual or psychosocial disability
  • Families needing extra support with routines and community access
  • Participants with complex support needs

We collaborate closely with support coordinators, plan managers, therapists and mainstream services to make sure you have a strong and supportive team around you.

Funding we work with

  • Self-managed NDIS participants
  • Plan-managed NDIS participants
  • Informal enquiries from families and carers

Where we support

We provide supports across Victoria, including:
