Community Participation & Social Access

Getting out into the community can be a big step. We provide calm, consistent support so you can attend appointments, activities, worship, learning and social outings with confidence.

Our workers move at your pace, respect your culture and communication style, and help make the community feel more accessible and welcoming.

  • Appointments, programs, shopping, worship & social activities
  • Support across Greater Melbourne, Geelong, Ballarat, Bendigo & Shepparton
  • Self-managed & plan-managed participants welcome

What Community Participation Can Include

We support you to access the community in ways that feel meaningful and safe to you ‚Äî not just ticking boxes, but building confidence and connection.

Appointments & essential trips

Support to attend medical, therapy, NDIS, legal or community appointments, including help with planning, navigating and communication.

Shopping & daily errands

Assistance with groceries, banking, post office visits, picking up medications and other everyday tasks in the community.

Social & recreational activities

Support to attend programs, classes, sports, community centres, events, hobbies and interest groups.

Faith, culture & community

Attending places of worship, cultural events, community gatherings and language-based groups in a way that feels respectful and safe.

Skill-building in the community

Practising money handling, public transport, wayfinding, ordering food, time management and other everyday skills.

NDIS goal-focused support

Aligning outings and activities with your NDIS goals around independence, social participation and community engagement.
