What Support Coordination Includes

We help you make the most of your NDIS plan with clarity and confidence.

Understanding your plan

We explain budgets, categories and what each part of your plan can fund.

Connecting you with providers

We match you with therapists, support workers and specialists that fit your needs.

Coordinating services

We help organise supports, manage schedules and ensure providers communicate well.

Preparing for reviews

We help gather reports, track progress and provide guidance for NDIS meetings.

Problem-solving

We help you resolve issues with providers, budgets or communication.

Capacity-building

We support you to build confidence and independence managing your plan over time.

How We Work With You

  1. 1. We get to know your needs

    Your culture, routines, goals and strengths guide how we coordinate your supports.

  2. 2. We create a clear coordination plan

    We identify the right providers and outline next steps in simple language.

  3. 3. We walk alongside you

    We check in regularly, adjust supports and help you prepare for reviews.
