Personal Care & Daily Living

Compassionate, respectful and culturally aware support delivered by trained professionals — helping you feel safe, confident and supported at home and in your daily routines.

What Personal Care Includes

Our team provides gentle, person-centred support tailored to your preferences, cultural needs and daily routines. Your dignity, privacy and comfort always come first.

  • Showering, bathing & personal hygiene
  • Dressing, grooming & appearance
  • Toileting & continence support
  • Mobility assistance & safe transfers
  • Medication prompts
  • Meal preparation & nutrition support
  • Morning & bedtime routines
  • Support with household tasks related to daily living

We take pride in safe, respectful care

Every person has unique preferences, routines and cultural needs. Our workers take time to understand what makes you feel comfortable, respected and supported.

Whether you need light assistance or full support, we will adapt our approach to make things easier, safer and more empowering.

Who Is This Service For?

Our Personal Care service is ideal for participants who need support with day-to-day living activities, whether occasionally, daily or throughout the week.

Adults

People with physical, psychosocial or neurological disabilities who need daily support.

Children & Teens

Young people who need safe, patient and understanding daily living assistance.

Ageing Participants

Those who require gentle, respectful assistance to maintain independence at home.

How We Deliver Personal Care

Personal care is one of the most sensitive types of support a participant receives. We approach every interaction with professionalism, empathy and cultural understanding.

  • Workers matched to your preferences
  • Respectful communication at all times
  • Clear routines to ensure consistency
  • Choice and control over how tasks are done
  • Support that encourages independence, not dependence

Safety & Quality You Can Trust

All personal care workers follow NDIS Quality and Safeguards guidelines. Your safety, privacy and dignity are our highest priority.

We also ensure compatibility — so you feel comfortable and confident with the people supporting you.

Worker Matching That Makes a Difference

The right support worker can transform your daily experience. We match you based on:

Personality

We ensure your worker is someone you feel comfortable and supported by.

Experience

We match workers with relevant skills — including complex care, mobility and communication support.

Cultural Understanding

Your routines, language, faith and cultural preferences always matter to us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I choose my support worker?

Yes. Worker matching is a key part of our service — you decide who supports you.

Do workers respect cultural needs?

Absolutely. We take culture, language, routines and family roles seriously.

Can I get help in the mornings and evenings?

Yes — we can support your regular routines at times that work for you.

Do you support complex personal care?

Yes. Many of our workers are trained in complex care and mobility support.

Is this service for children?

Yes — we support children, teens, adults and aging participants.
