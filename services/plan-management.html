What Our Plan Management Service Is Aimed to Provide

Once available, Able Aide plan management is being designed to offer:

Invoice processing

Managing and paying approved NDIS invoices from registered and unregistered providers, in line with NDIS rules.

Budget tracking

Helping you understand how your plan funds are being used across different categories.

Spending summaries

Clear, regular statements showing what has been spent, what remains and any trends to be aware of.

Provider support

Working with your providers so invoices are accurate, timely and within NDIS guidelines.

NDIS compliance guidance

Helping you understand what the NDIS will and won‚Äôt fund, and referring you back to your planner or LAC where needed.
