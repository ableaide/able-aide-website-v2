About Able Aide

We are committed to delivering person-centred, culturally respectful, and high-quality disability support to participants and families across Victoria.

Our Story

Able Aide was founded with one purpose — to provide reliable, respectful, and compassionate disability support that makes a real difference in people’s lives. We believe that every person deserves support that honours their culture, identity, choices, and independence.

Our services are built around trust, consistency, and genuine relationships. Whether we’re supporting daily living, coordinating services, helping families with routines, or assisting participants to build confidence in the community — we put people first.

Able Aide proudly supports participants across Victoria including Melbourne, Geelong, Ballarat, Bendigo, Shepparton, and surrounding regional communities.

Syeda Zahra

Founder & Director

Syeda has over 10 years of experience in the disability sector, including support work, care coordination, family advocacy, and community engagement. Her vision for Able Aide is rooted in compassion, dignity, and cultural understanding.

Our Values

Respect

We honour your culture, language, routines, and identity — ensuring support feels safe and inclusive.

Consistency

You deserve familiar faces. We prioritise long-term worker relationships and stable supports.

Empowerment

Our goal is to support you to live the life you choose — with confidence, independence, and dignity.

Cultural Understanding

Your faith, values, and family structure matter to us. We tailor supports around what is meaningful to you.

Quality & Safety

All services follow NDIS Quality & Safeguarding standards to ensure safe, compliant, high-quality care.

Collaboration

We work closely with families, therapists, support coordinators, and the wider care network.
