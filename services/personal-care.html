Personal Care & Daily Living Support

Personal care is one of the most intimate and important types of support a person can receive. At Able Aide, we provide gentle, respectful and culturally aware assistance with daily routines, hygiene, mobility, home tasks and wellbeing — always at your pace and according to your comfort.

You choose the routines, the timing, and how you like things done. Our role is to support you with dignity, safety and compassion.

Support that feels safe, predictable and empowering

We understand how sensitive personal care can be. That’s why we focus on consistency, communication and building trust — so every interaction feels calm and respectful.

Talk to us about your support needs

Support Designed Around Your Daily Life

Everyday tasks can take a lot of energy — physically, emotionally or mentally. Our personal care support is designed to ease routines, increase comfort and promote independence. We never rush, and we never take over where you want to stay involved.

What We Can Help With

Personal Hygiene & Care

Gentle support with:

  • Showering or bathing
  • Toileting & continence care
  • Oral hygiene
  • Grooming, haircare & shaving
  • Skin care & basic pressure care (within scope)
  • Hand hygiene & infection control

Dressing, Mobility & Comfort

Assistance with daily physical needs, including:

  • Getting dressed & undressed
  • Choosing weather-appropriate clothing
  • Positioning & transfers
  • Using mobility equipment safely
  • Supporting muscle comfort & posture

Daily Living & Home Routines

Helping your home feel safe, organised and comfortable:

  • Meal preparation
  • Light domestic tasks (dishes, making beds, tidying)
  • Laundry & linen changes
  • Medication reminders (within scope)
  • Following therapy strategies at home

Our Approach to Personal Care

We believe high-quality personal care is not only about tasks — it’s about dignity, communication and trust.

1

We Listen First

Before we support you, we learn your preferences, routines, cultural needs, language, communication style and boundaries.

2

We Work at Your Pace

Support is never rushed. You set the pace and can stop, change or adjust the routine at any time.

3

We Provide Consistency

Where possible, we send the same support workers so your routines feel predictable, familiar and comfortable.

Who This Service Is For

Personal care support may be suitable if:

  • You need physical assistance with hygiene or mobility
  • You’re recovering from a health event
  • You experience fatigue, pain or limited mobility
  • You live alone and want support with routines
  • You need help building independence at home
  • Parents or carers need respite or shared supports

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I choose my own support worker?

Yes. We prioritise matching based on personality, communication style, gender preference and cultural needs.

Can I change workers if I feel uncomfortable?

Absolutely. Your comfort comes first — you can request changes at any time.

Are supports available 7 days a week?

Yes, personal care can be arranged mornings, evenings, weekends and public holidays based on availability.

Is personal care funded by the NDIS?

Yes, personal care is funded as a Core Support under “Assistance with Daily Life.”

Do you support people with complex care needs?

We support many participants with higher needs. For specialised nursing tasks, we can coordinate with clinical providers.

Can you help with therapy routines?

Yes — we can follow strategies and recommendations from your OT, physio or speech therapist.

Do I need to clean before workers arrive?

No. We understand real-life homes. Light domestic support is included if needed.

Will workers speak my language?

Where possible, yes. We will always respect cultural, faith and communication needs.

Can you support children?

Yes, we assist children and families with respectful, age-appropriate support.

Can you support me during hospital discharge?

Yes — we can coordinate personal care routines during transitions home.

Need Respectful Help With Daily Living?

We know that inviting someone into your home is personal. We’re happy to begin with a gentle, no-pressure conversation.
