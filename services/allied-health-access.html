Allied Health Access Support

Finding therapists, managing waitlists and navigating NDIS requirements can feel overwhelming. We help participants and families access the right allied health services — calmly and clearly.

Whether you need OT, physiotherapy, psychology, speech therapy, behaviour support or assessments, we support the process from start to finish.

Support connecting with therapists who understand your goals

We help bridge the gap between recommendations and real life.

Get Help Accessing Allied Health Services

Making Allied Health Easier to Navigate

Allied health support often involves multiple providers, forms, referrals, waitlists and therapy plans. We help make it manageable, organised and aligned with your goals.

We are not a therapy clinic — our role is to support you to access the right therapists, stay on top of appointments, and bring therapy strategies into your daily routines and home environment.

How We Support Allied Health Access

Finding the Right Therapists

  • Occupational Therapists (OT)
  • Physiotherapists
  • Speech Pathologists
  • Psychologists & counsellors
  • Behaviour Support Practitioners
  • Functional, sensory & developmental assessors

Appointments, Referrals & Waitlists

  • Enquiries & navigating waitlists
  • Referral forms (within scope)
  • Booking & confirming appointments
  • Coordinating transport & support workers
  • Tracking reports & follow-ups

Implementing Therapy Strategies

  • Supporting routines recommended by therapists
  • Breaking tasks into step-by-step plans
  • Using simple prompts or visuals
  • Supporting safe use of equipment
  • Preparing for NDIS reassessments

Who This Service Is For

Allied Health Access may be ideal if you:

  • Don't know where to find therapists
  • Are stuck on long therapy waitlists
  • Feel overwhelmed by reports or recommendations
  • Struggle managing multiple appointments
  • Need help implementing therapy goals
  • Have upcoming NDIS reviews that need assessments

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you an allied health clinic?

No — we support you to access therapists, but we do not deliver therapy.

Can you recommend therapists?

Yes — based on your needs, funding, cultural preferences and location.

Can you book appointments?

Yes — we support enquiries, bookings, and coordinating schedules.

Do you attend therapy sessions?

Yes, where appropriate, helpful and funded.

Can you help with NDIS reports?

We help track due dates. Therapists write the clinical reports.

Do you assist with equipment & AT?

We help with logistics — clinical decisions remain with therapists.

What if I already have therapists?

We coordinate, organise and help integrate strategies into routines.

Do you support participants with limited English?

Yes — we work with interpreters and culturally appropriate providers.

Is this funded under NDIS?

Typically funded under Capacity Building or Core Supports.

How do I start?

Contact us directly or ask your Support Coordinator to send a referral.
