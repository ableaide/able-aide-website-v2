Privacy Policy

Able Aide respects your privacy, your culture, your boundaries and your choices. We take care to protect your information and comply with Australian privacy law and NDIS Practice Standards.

Your privacy matters

We only collect information needed to provide safe, high-quality NDIS support. You can request access, updates or corrections at any time.

Ask a privacy question

1. About This Policy

This policy explains how Able Aide collects, uses, stores and protects your personal information. It applies to all services we provide across Victoria.

2. What Personal Information We Collect

2.1 Participant Information

  • Name, DOB and contact details
  • NDIS number and funding type
  • Suburb and preferred service times
  • Health or disability-related information
  • Cultural or language needs
  • Therapy recommendations (with consent)
  • Risk, behaviour or safety considerations
  • Emergency contact details

2.2 Referrer Information

  • Name and organisation
  • Role or relationship to participant
  • Email and phone number
  • Information provided during referral

2.3 Website & Form Information

  • Form submissions
  • Basic usage analytics
  • Standard cookies

3. Why We Collect Information

  • Provide safe, person-centred NDIS support
  • Understand your goals and needs
  • Match you with suitable workers
  • Communicate with you and your support team
  • Meet NDIS compliance and safety obligations
  • Improve our services
  • Respond to enquiries, referrals or incidents

4. How We Collect Information

We collect information through referrals, forms, phone calls, emails, meetings, support coordination discussions and website analytics.

Where possible, we collect information directly from you. Information from others requires your consent, unless there is a serious safety risk.

5. Sensitive & Health Information

As an NDIS provider, we handle sensitive information with the highest level of confidentiality. We comply with the Australian Privacy Act and NDIS Practice Standards.

6. How We Store & Protect Your Information

  • Encrypted digital systems
  • Strict access controls
  • Secure cloud-based NDIS-compliant storage
  • Password-protected devices
  • Confidentiality agreements
  • Regular privacy and safety training

7. When We Share Information

We never sell your information.

  • You give consent
  • To deliver safe support
  • Required by law
  • To prevent a serious risk of harm
  • NDIS audit or compliance requirements

8. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your information
  • Ask to update or correct information
  • Withdraw consent
  • Know how your information is used
  • Make a privacy complaint
  • Use an advocate

9. Children & Young People

For participants under 18, information is collected with guardian consent unless the young person can provide informed consent themselves.

10. Interpreters & Cultural Support

We can arrange interpreters or adjust communication styles to support language, culture or faith preferences.

11. Website Cookies & Analytics

We use minimal analytics and standard cookies for website functionality. We do not use advertising trackers.

12. Privacy Questions & Complaints

You can contact us anytime:

Email: enquiries@ableaide.com.au

Phone: 0435 039 110

If unresolved, you can contact the OAIC or NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission.

13. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy when laws or NDIS standards change. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Have Questions About Privacy?

We’re happy to explain anything in this policy in clear, simple language.
